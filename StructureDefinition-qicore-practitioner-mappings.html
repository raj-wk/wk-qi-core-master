Mappings for Quality Improvement Domain Analysis Model (http://www.hl7.org/implement/standards/product_brief.cfm?product_id=378)

QICore-Practitioner
Practitioner
   extension (classification)Not in QIDAM, but recognized as important. While there is practitionerRole, that may refer more to something like “attending” vs. “consultant” physician, whereas the classification would be useful for classifying the practitioner to things like “Physician”, “Resident”, “Pharmacist”, “Nurse”, “PA”. This could then determine whether the practitioner should receive a particular CDS.
